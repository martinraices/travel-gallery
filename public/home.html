Pepini per il mondo

Pepini per il mondo is a private travel gallery for invited users to organize travel albums, photos, and videos.

Users can sign in with Google, create private or shared albums, upload media, and optionally connect YouTube to upload user-selected videos to their own YouTube account as private videos.

YouTube access is used only for user-initiated video upload actions and optional deletion of videos that the user chooses to remove from YouTube through the app.
