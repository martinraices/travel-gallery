Pepini per il mondo Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 10, 2026

Overview

Pepini per il mondo is a private travel gallery used to organize, upload, and share travel photos and videos. The app is operated by Martin Raices and is intended for invited users and guests with shared links.

Information We Collect

When you sign in with Google, we receive basic account information such as your email address, display name, and Google user identifier. Inside the app, users may add profile details, album metadata, photos, videos, descriptions, city labels, and sharing preferences.

Google and YouTube API Use

If you choose to connect YouTube, the app requests YouTube permissions for uploading private videos and, when you explicitly choose it, deleting those uploaded videos from YouTube.

The app does not read your YouTube channel content, does not publish videos publicly, and does not sell or share YouTube API data with third parties. YouTube access tokens are kept in browser session storage and are used only during the active upload flow.

Pepini per il mondo's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

How We Use Information

We use app data to provide the travel gallery experience: authentication, album creation, media upload, private or shared album access, profile settings, notifications, and optional YouTube private video upload.

Sharing

Albums are shared only according to the settings selected in the app, such as guest visibility, specific invited email addresses, or public share links. We do not sell personal information.

Storage and Security

Photos, videos, album data, and profile settings are stored using Firebase services. Access is controlled by Firebase Authentication and security rules that limit users to the albums and files they own or have been granted access to.

Revoking Access

You can disconnect the app from your Google account at any time from your Google Account permissions page: https://myaccount.google.com/permissions.

Contact

For privacy questions or access requests, contact: raicesm@gmail.com.
